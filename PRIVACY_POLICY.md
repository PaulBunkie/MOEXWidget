# Privacy Policy for MOEX Widget

**Last Updated: June 23, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the MOEX Widget application.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect
MOEX Widget is designed to provide financial market data (stock and cryptocurrency prices) via a home screen widget. We believe in your privacy, therefore:
- We **do not collect** any personal identification information (such as name, email address, or phone number).
- We **do not track** your location.
- We **do not access** your contacts, photos, or other personal files on your device.

## 2. Data Usage and Connectivity
To provide real-time or historical price data, the application connects to public financial APIs (such as MOEX, Yahoo Finance, or Binance). 
- These requests are made directly from your device to the data providers.
- No intermediary server owned by us processes your requests or stores your financial interests.

## 3. Data Storage
The application uses local storage (Room database) on your device to cache historical candle data for the widgets you have created. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to any external servers by us.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The app may use third-party financial data providers. Each provider has its own privacy policy regarding how they handle the technical data (like IP addresses) required to fulfill an API request. We recommend reviewing their policies:
- [Moscow Exchange (MOEX)](https://www.moex.com/)
- [Yahoo Finance](https://legal.yahoo.com/us/en/yahoo/privacy/index.html)
- [Binance](https://www.binance.com/en/privacy)

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our application does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository or the contact information provided in the store.
